Key German Labor Laws

Working Hours: Max 8/day, 40/week. Overtime compensated.

Vacation: Minimum 20 days/year (most offer 25-30).

Sick Leave: 6 weeks paid, then 70% statutory benefit.

Notice Period: Default 4 weeks to 15th/end of month. Probation: 2 weeks.

Severance: Not mandatory unless mutually agreed. Calculate: 0.5-1.0 × monthly salary × years.

Taxes: Income tax + solidarity (5.5%) + church (8-9%) + social security (~20%).
